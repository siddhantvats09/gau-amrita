Bilona Vidhi

The Sacred
6-Step Process

Every jar of Gau Amrita takes 72+ hours to prepare. No machine shortcuts. Just pure, patient craftsmanship passed through generations.

𓃖

Fresh A2 Milk Collection

Every morning at sunrise, fresh milk is collected from our Gir and Sahiwal cows by hand. Only cows that have recently calved are milked — ensuring A2 beta-casein rich milk.

01
02
⏾⋆.

Traditional Curd Preparation

Warm milk is set to curd using a small portion of the previous batch. Left overnight under natural conditions — no artificial cultures, no temperature manipulation.

꩜

Hand Churning — Bilona

The curd is hand-churned using a wooden churner (mathani) in a clay pot. This ancient method separates cream while preserving all natural nutrients and probiotic properties.

03
04
♨

Slow Heating on Wood Fire

The collected butter is slowly heated in heavy brass vessels on a low wood fire. This slow cook evaporates all moisture and creates the characteristic nutty aroma of pure ghee.

⚕

Purity Testing

Each batch undergoes the traditional Butyro-Refractometer test and modern lab testing for moisture content, fatty acid profile, and absence of adulterants before packaging.

05
06
⛟

Hygienic Packaging

Packed in BPA-free glass jars in our certified clean room facility. Sealed and shipped directly to your home — farm fresh, no middleman, no compromise.

Certified Pure,
Tested & Trusted

Every batch is independently verified. Because you deserve to know exactly what goes into your body and your family's food.
